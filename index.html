The Book Basement | FireBase !

The Project Context Is :

The main objective of The Book Basement aims to create an Best Online Book Selling Store that allows users to search and purchase a book online based on three elements : The Title, the Author and the Subject. The Book Basement contains the Latest , Upcoming Listings & the Hotest Books of the Week. The selected books are displayed in a tabular format and the user can order their books online through credit card payment. Using this Website the user can purchase a book online instead of going out to a book store and wasting time.

The Problem Statement Is :

The Main issue that people and in particular students faces different types of difficulties regarding their time management combined to the actual mobility issues. Here they can't visit the Book Store and find the books they were looking for.

The Solution Is :

As people faces many issues like Time and Travelling , and people do not go to book stores anymore. I decided to launch an online bookstore. Through this Website, People buy books online according to their niches without any difficulties.

Benefits To Users Are :

There are several advantages that come with purchasing books online some which include;

Convinience : When the User shop books online, the User save a lot of time which they would have wasted wandering from one store to another if you were to shop at the conventional bookstore.
It is Easy to Access Consumer Reviews : When Users shop online, users have the chance of accessing and going through customers who have shopped at that store and review about the book you intend to buy. This will give you beforehand information about the book.
No Limited Choice : Ordinary stores have limited space to display books, but The Book Basement has huge databases, and users can hardly miss finding the book you are looking for.

The Target Audience Is :

Everyone can buy a Book from The Book Basement. Like , Students , Teachers & People that belongs to any field.
